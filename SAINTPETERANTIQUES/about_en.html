About us

St. Peter Antiques specializes in fine arts and antiques.
In the area where we offer services we organize an international center for logistics of antique furniture.
The items range from extremely rare, worthy of collections to decorative antiques, ideal for home decoration. Established in the market and trade of St. Peter Antiques we have established a reputation for integrity in business in addition to well-selected and researched art.

In 2021, St. Peter Antiques has expanded our business due to the growing interests of consumers and we work exclusively online, but you can make an appointment and discuss all issues from the purchase of items in the country and abroad to their delivery. Our website is updated frequently and you can follow all the news on it. Our participation and the contacts we have established in different parts of the world allow us to maintain relatively low prices and discounts at unprecedented levels for wholesale orders. We try to satisfy every taste and requirements.
